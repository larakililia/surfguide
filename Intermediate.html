Wow already an intermediate surfer?



Unlike what a lot of people think, the surf is really really exhausting and hard,I am on clear ground. Wathever, here is the maneuvers that you have to learn for this level:

The duck dive:

One of the most important thing to know in surf when you pass the beginner level is the duck dive. It allows you to pass under the waves with your board, and continuing to go foward even with the breaking wavees. It is a basic and extreme important meneuver,the key to access to the wide. Here are the main maneuvers to master to move to the next level :

The bottom:

The bottom turn is a smooth, powerful turn at the bottom of the wave that transforms vertical energy (gained by dropping the wave), into horizontal energy (turning at the bottom), in a way that provides you the proper speed and direction for a manoeuvre on the wave.





The roller:

The roller is a turn on the top of the wave. The risk is being surprised by the strenght of the turn. In fact, the effect is incredible. The pivot of the transfer of support multiply the rotation of the board.





The floater:

A floater is when a surfer go on the top of a broken wave. (This part is called the lip .) This maneuver is called floater because surfeurs have the impression of floating on a cloud.





The cutback:

A cutback is when a surfer changes of direction thanks to a turn, by using his rails to get back to the pocket. It is a crucial maneuver, it places the surfer next to the strenght source; the abrupter part, and the most pleasant of the wave.





The barrel:

A barrel is an extreme sportive figure of surf. It consists at surfing inside of a breaking wave, with the shape of a tunnel.



If you manage all those maneuvers,you passed the intermediate level, you are now officially a Professional.
