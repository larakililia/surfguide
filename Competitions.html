Competitions?


For the international competitions, they are organised by the WSL (World Surf League), an american buisness. The conduct of the competiton is the following: 34 mans 22 womans They make rounds to qualify themselves. Just like for a soccer competition. At the end, only one will have the opportunity of brandishing the cup. Their aim is to earn a maximum of points during their performances. The judgeds pay attention to differents critaries:

  • The engagment and the level of difficulty.
  • Inonvants and progressives maneuvers.
  • Combinaisons of several maneuvers.
  • Variety of the maneuvers.
  • The speed, the strength and the chain.

The concurrents have one heat (30minutes) to demonstrate their talent. They often surf with an other concurrent, so, they have to respect some priority rules.The judgeds will note them on a scale from 0 to 10 and will only add the two surfer’s best performances for the final result. Quality is better than quantity.
